Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak season (July-October and December-February). However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What's included in the package price?

All our packages include: 4x4 safari vehicle, professional guide, park entrance fees, accommodation, all meals, bottled water, and game drives. International flights, visas, travel insurance, and personal expenses are not included.

Is it safe to travel on safari?

Yes! Safari tourism is very safe when conducted with experienced guides. We follow all safety protocols, stay in secure accommodations, and our guides are trained in first aid and emergency response. We also provide comprehensive safety briefings before each safari.

What should I pack?

Essentials include: neutral-colored clothing (khaki, brown, green), comfortable walking shoes, sun protection (hat, sunscreen, sunglasses), camera with zoom lens, binoculars, insect repellent, and any personal medications. We'll send a detailed packing list after booking.

Can you customize packages?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your interests, timeline, and budget. Want to combine multiple destinations? Extend your stay? Add cultural experiences? Just let us know your preferences.

What's the best time to visit?

Kenya offers year-round safari opportunities! July-October is peak season for the Great Migration. January-February offers excellent game viewing with newborn animals. The "green season" (April-June) has fewer crowds and lush landscapes. We'll help you choose based on your priorities.
