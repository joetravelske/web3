Maasai Mara Classic

Experience the iconic Maasai Mara National Reserve, home to the Big Five and the spectacular Great Migration. This is Kenya's most famous safari destination.

  • 3 Days / 2 Nights
  • Game drives twice daily
  • Witness the Big Five
  • Great Migration season (July-October)
  • Visit Maasai villages
  • Expert local guide
  • Full board accommodation
  • 4x4 Safari vehicle

Package Highlights:

The Maasai Mara is renowned for its exceptional population of lions, leopards, cheetahs, and elephants. During migration season, witness over 1.5 million wildebeest crossing the Mara River. Cultural visits to authentic Maasai villages provide insight into traditional pastoral life.

Amboseli Adventure

Discover Amboseli National Park with its stunning backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest peak. Famous for large elephant herds and diverse birdlife.

  • 2 Days / 1 Night
  • Mount Kilimanjaro views
  • Large elephant herds
  • Over 400 bird species
  • Observation Hill panoramic views
  • Swamp wildlife viewing
  • Photography paradise
  • Comfortable lodge stay

Package Highlights:

Amboseli offers some of the best opportunities to see elephants up close against the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro. The park's diverse ecosystems include wetlands with hippos, savannah with zebras and wildebeest, and acacia woodlands. Perfect for photographers and nature lovers.

Tsavo Safari

Explore Kenya's largest national park, divided into Tsavo East and West. Known for red elephants, dramatic landscapes, and the historic Man-Eaters of Tsavo.

  • 4 Days / 3 Nights
  • Tsavo East & West parks
  • Famous red elephants
  • Mzima Springs viewing
  • Lugard Falls
  • Shetani Lava Flows
  • Less crowded experience
  • Extended game viewing

Package Highlights:

Tsavo's vast wilderness offers a raw, authentic safari experience. The red dust gives elephants their distinctive color. Mzima Springs is a crystal-clear oasis where you can see hippos and crocodiles underwater through viewing platforms. Discover the rich history of the "Man-Eaters of Tsavo" and enjoy spectacular sunsets over endless landscapes.
